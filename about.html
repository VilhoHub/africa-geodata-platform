About Africa Geoscience Data Platform (AGDP)

Empowering Africa through accessible and comprehensive geospatial data.

The Africa Geoscience Data Platform (AGDP) is a comprehensive digital repository and interactive portal designed to make geospatial data and resources readily accessible to stakeholders across Africa. Our mission is to foster innovation, support decision-making, and drive sustainable development across the continent through the effective use of geoscience data.

Our Mission

AGDP aims to provide a unified platform for accessing, sharing, and utilizing geospatial data to support diverse initiatives, including infrastructure development, environmental conservation, disaster management, and socio-economic planning.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive geospatial data inventory covering various themes such as climate, hydrology, transportation networks, and more.
  • Access to ongoing, completed, and upcoming African geospatial projects.
  • Integration with existing geospatial platforms to expand the reach and usability of data.
  • User-friendly tools for data visualization, analysis, and decision-making.

Collaborators and Stakeholders

We collaborate with government agencies, academic institutions, private organizations, and international bodies to ensure the availability and accuracy of geoscience data. Our stakeholders include policymakers, researchers, businesses, and local communities striving for impactful solutions.

Data Themes

The AGDP organizes its data into themes, each addressing specific areas of interest:

  • Administrative Boundaries
  • Climate and Weather
  • Disaster Risk Management
  • Hydrology and Inland Water
  • Transportation Networks
  • And many more...

Why Choose AGDP?

AGDP is more than a platform; it is a movement towards a data-driven future for Africa. By bridging the gap between data producers and users, AGDP empowers everyone to make informed decisions for a better tomorrow.
