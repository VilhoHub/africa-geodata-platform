Platform Highlights

  • Africa GeoPortal: Based in Kenya, providing tools for GIS mapping and analysis.
  • RCMRD: Hosted in South Africa, specializing in regional geospatial data.
  • Dunia: Based in Tanzania, offering a platform for localized data management.
  • ArcGIS (Esri Eastern Africa): Uganda, with extensive geospatial toolkits.
  • African Geomatics Namibia: Namibia, providing geomatics and geospatial services.
  • Botswana Geoscience Portal: Botswana, focusing on geological and mineral data.
